About Me

Data Scientist · IT Professional · Nairobi, Kenya

I'm Dorcas Kabutie — a BSc Information Technology graduate from Kabarak University, currently completing the Moringa School Data Science Bootcamp. I care about what data can do for people, especially here in Kenya and across Africa.

My time at the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) shaped how I think about technology. Working at Kenya's primary data privacy institution taught me that behind every dataset is a person — and that protecting data is protecting dignity.

My flagship project, AdolescentMind, is the clearest expression of that belief. It uses machine learning to predict anxiety and depression among high school students in Kenya — not as an academic exercise, but as a tool school counsellors can actually use. Data should lead to action.
